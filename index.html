About

The Story

A self-taught guitarist with a deep love for Tamil & Hindi film music. Every cover is a tribute to the composers who shaped generations — AR Rahman, Ilaiyaraaja, Harris Jayaraj, Vishal–Shekhar, and more.

The goal is simple: make beautiful music accessible. Watch, learn, and let your fingers find the same melodies that move your soul.

All tabs are personally transcribed — not auto-generated — ensuring accuracy, playability, and every nuance of the original captured.
